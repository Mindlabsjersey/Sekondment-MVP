# SEKONDMENT EXPERTISE EXPANSION — Strategic Brief

**What this is:** A curated set of 62 expertise records (across 14 sectors) grounded in current market demand for fractional, project-based, and specialist work. Ready to inject into the live app.

**Who this is for:** Companies and teams needing expertise on-demand, without hiring. Specialists offering fractional leadership or project-shaped work.

---

## The use cases that fit Sekondment best

### 1. **Financial services & compliance** (highest immediacy)
**Why:** Regulatory deadlines, spiky workload, zero tolerance for delay.
- **AML/KYC reviews** — a business faces a deadline, needs a compliance expert for 2 weeks, can't justify hiring.
- **Fund administration overflow** — a fund manager gets a surge of new vehicles, needs interim admins to clear the backlog.
- **Trust administration** — an executor needs specialist help managing a trust, engagement ends in 6 months.
- **Regulatory reporting** — year-end, tax filing, audit prep — bring in expertise just-in-time.

**Expertise in scope:** AML Review, KYC Review, Trust Administration, Fund Administration, Regulatory Reporting, Financial Modelling, Fractional CFO, Fractional Financial Controller.

### 2. **Tech & SaaS scale-ups** (rapid growth, focused gaps)
**Why:** Growth is uneven; expertise needs are temporary but critical.
- **Stripe Connect build** — a marketplace is starting payouts; hire a Stripe specialist for 3 months, ship it, move on.
- **Fractional CTO** — a founder needs technical leadership but can't afford a full CTO yet; deploy a fractional CTO on retainer.
- **Cloud architecture / migration** — moving to AWS, need an architect to design the migration; project ends when it's done.
- **Kubernetes / platform engineering** — scaling containerisation; hire a platform engineer for 6 months to set it up right.
- **ISO27001** — security audit is coming, build the controls before it lands.

**Expertise in scope:** Cloud Architecture (AWS/Azure), Kubernetes, SRE, Terraform, Stripe Connect, Microsoft 365 Migration, Fractional CTO, ISO27001 Implementation, SOC2 Readiness.

### 3. **Cybersecurity** (universal, highest rate, biggest shortage)
**Why:** 4M+ global shortage. Every org needs it. High stakes = premium rates.
- **Penetration testing** — annual compliance, pre-launch security check.
- **Fractional CISO** — a company doesn't need a full-time CISO, but needs a senior security leader part-time. Engagement: 2 days/week, strategic guidance.
- **Incident response** — breach happened, need forensics and containment *now*. Project-shaped work.
- **Zero Trust implementation** — multi-month transformation, need an architect + implementation lead.

**Expertise in scope:** Penetration Testing, Security Architecture, Incident Response, Zero Trust, Cloud Security, AI Security / Red Teaming, SOC2 Readiness, ISO27001, Fractional CISO.

### 4. **AI & Data** (emergent, highest growth)
**Why:** Everyone wants AI. Few have the talent. Fractional deployment makes sense.
- **ML engineering** — a company wants to build a predictive model; hire an ML engineer for the project lifecycle.
- **Fractional AI Officer** — a business needs AI strategy + governance but can't hire a full-time exec; bring in a fractional AI Officer to build the framework.
- **Data engineering** — data warehouse build-out; project-shaped engagement.
- **Data governance** — regulatory push (GDPR/data residency); need a governance expert to design the system.
- **LLM/Prompt engineering** — building an LLM-powered feature; hire a specialist for 3 months.

**Expertise in scope:** Machine Learning Engineering, Agentic AI Development, LLM / Prompt Engineering, MLOps, AI Governance & Ethics, Data Engineering, Data Governance, Analytics Engineering, Fractional AI Officer.

### 5. **Energy & ESG** (regulatory push, high-value)
**Why:** COP26, net-zero commitments, carbon accounting mandates drive urgent demand.
- **Carbon accounting & ESG reporting** — a company committed to net-zero, needs expertise to measure baseline and track.
- **Renewable energy project management** — building a solar/wind project, need interim PM expertise.
- **Grid integration** — utility company deploying distributed energy; need a specialist to manage the technical integration.

**Expertise in scope:** Renewable Energy Project Management, Carbon Accounting & ESG Reporting, Grid Integration.

### 6. **Construction & HSE** (compliance-heavy, premium)
**Why:** Regulations are strict. HSE violations are costly. Specialist knowledge commands premium rates.

- **Quantity surveying** — project-specific expertise; bring in a QS to track costs + variations.

**Expertise in scope:** HSE / Health & Safety Management, Quantity Surveying, BIM Coordination.

### 7. **Healthcare** (regulatory, highest-stakes)

## Why Sekondment is the right fit for these

1. **Spiky work** — engagements have start and end dates. Experts aren't hired permanently.
2. **Specialist expertise** — the work requires deep domain knowledge, not generalist skills.
3. **Regulated or high-stakes** — compliance, security, legal, clinical — where failure is costly.
4. **Geographic flexibility** — many of these roles don't require on-site presence (FSI, cyber, data, AI).
5. **Company Resource model advantage** — an employer can deploy their spare capacity (e.g., a bank's off-the-desk compliance officer) and earn on it.

## What happens when you inject this

1. **Businesses can post opportunities** requiring any of the 62 expertises.
2. **Experts can claim expertise** on their profiles (structured, verified, proven).
3. **The matching engine ranks** experts holding the required expertise against requirements.
4. **The Ops Centre** charts demand/supply for all 62, guiding recruitment and strategy.
5. **The taxonomy** becomes a **moat** — the more verified expertise you accumulate, the harder it is for competitors to replicate the marketplace's intelligence.
